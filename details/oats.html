Oats

Description

Why Oats?

Studies show that oats and oatmeal have many heatly benefits. These include weight loss, lower blood sugar levels, and a reduced risk of heart disease. If made with the right recipe its very tasty too!

Ingredients

  • 3 cups water
  • 1 cup steel-cut oats
  • Â¼ teaspoon sea salt
  • Cinnamon, nutmeg, and/or cardamom, optional
  • Honey or maple syrup, optional

The Recipe

  1. Bring the water to a boil in a medium pot. Add the oats and salt and reduce the heat to medium-low.
  2. Cook, stirring occasionally, for 15 to 20 minutes, or until thickened. Stir in a pinch of cinnamon, nutmeg, and/or cardamom, if desired.
  3. Remove from heat and let stand for two minutes.
  4. Serve hot with honey or maple syrup, if desired. To make the Apple & Tart Cherry variation, top with sliced apples, tart cherries, and almonds.
  5. To make the Blueberry & Coconut variation, top with blueberries, orange zest, a scoop of Greek yogurt, chia seeds, and coconut flakes.
