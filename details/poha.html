Poha

Description

What is Poha?

Poha is flattened rice that is steam cooked with onions, spices and herbs. The word "poha" refers to the flattened rice itself and as well the dish made with it. It is eaten for breakfast, snack and even for a quick dinner.

Ingredients

  • Medium flakes Poha
  • Peanuts
  • herbs
  • 1 onion
  • 2 green chillies
  • Spices and Salt
  • half a lemon

The Recipe

  1. Clean one cup poha and add them to a large colander, strainer or bowl. Don't use thin flakes. Medium recommended.

  2. Rinse them well with clean filitered water. Drain the water completely.

  3. Meanwhile chop 1 onion and 2 green chillies. Also rinse the curry and coriander leaves. Fine chop the coriander

  4. Heat the pan with 2 tablespoon oil and fry peanuts until golden and crunchy. Set them aside and heat the same pan again.

  5. When it gets hot add a bit more than half a tablespoon of mustard seeds and equal amount of cumin seeds. Let them splutter.

  6. Add the chopped onions, green chillies and curry leaves. Saute until the onions turn pink. Then add some turmeric(haldi). Mix and let it cook for a minute.

  7. Add the softened poha with peanuts and mix it all well. Then add half spoon red chilli powder, 1 tablespoon dhaniya powder, 1/4 tablespoon garam masala and finally salt as per taste. Let it cook for a minute or two.

  8. Take it off. Add lemon juice and mix it well. Garnish it with coriander leaves and peanuts
